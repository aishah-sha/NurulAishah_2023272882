Japan Travel

Japan offers a blend of tradition and futuristic cities.

Top destinations include Tokyo, Kyoto, and Osaka.

Best time to visit: March–May (cherry blossoms)

Popular foods include sushi, ramen, and takoyaki.

Language Tip: "Arigato" means thank you
  • Visit Mt. Fuji
  • Explore Kyoto temples
  • Try a Japanese onsen
France Travel

France is famous for its art, fashion, and cuisine.

Don't miss Paris, Provence, and the French Riviera.

Best time to visit: April–June or September–November
  • See the Eiffel Tower
  • Tour the Louvre
  • Relax in Nice
Australia Travel

Known for beaches, wildlife, and the Outback.

Highlights include Sydney, Melbourne, and the Great Barrier Reef.

Driving: Left side of the road Currency: Australian Dollar (AUD)
Italy Travel

Italy combines ancient history and delicious food.

Popular cities: Rome, Venice, Florence.

Must-try dish: Margherita Pizza in Naples
Budget Tips

Travel off-season for cheaper flights.

Use public transport and travel cards.

Check for city passes that include transport + attractions
  • Pack light
  • Book early
  • Compare accommodation platforms
